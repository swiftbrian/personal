TREMproof 200 MA — Seam Specs
Self-adhered modified bitumen membrane
Min overlap 3 inches — Tremco warranty requirement, non-negotiable

Probe seam every 12" with seam probe tool — Rosa logs all readings on ITP sheet
Below 50°F: warm membrane with heat gun before seaming — cold membrane won't bond
Never seam over a wrinkle or lifted section — cut out and patch
